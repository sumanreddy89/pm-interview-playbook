# Monetization Framework

## What This Question Tests
Tests how analytical and creative you are in devising monetization models. Interviewers want to see structured thinking, user-segment awareness, and creative revenue ideas.

## Evaluation Criteria
- Do you identify a clear monetization goal?
- Do you identify the best segments to target?
- Do you explore what segment needs are monetizable?
- Are your monetization ideas creative and effective?
- Structured and clear communication?

---

## The Framework

### Step 1: Clarify
Ask: Does the business/product already exist? This constrains your options — you can't change the core product if it's live.

### Step 2: State Your Goal
Define what kind of monetization model you're targeting:
- Low risk / low capital investment
- Minimal product changes needed
- Maximize margins
- Maximize reach/adoption first (freemium)
- Recurring revenue vs. one-time

### Step 3: Preview Your Approach
Tell the interviewer: "I'll identify the best segments to target, brainstorm what they'd pay for, and recommend the best model."

### Step 4: User Segments
Segment by characteristics that separate groups with different needs and willingness to pay:
- Income level, job type, lifestyle, use frequency
- Create 2–3 distinct personas
- Prioritize based on: demand size, willingness to pay, ease of monetizing

### Step 5: Monetizable Needs
For each prioritized segment, identify what they'd pay for:
- Premium features / tiers
- Time savings
- Convenience
- Status / exclusivity
- Access to data or insights
- Physical goods or services

### Step 6: Monetization Models
Common models to consider:

| Model | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Subscription** | Recurring fee for ongoing access |
| **Freemium** | Free basic tier + paid premium features |
| **Pay-per-use** | Charge per transaction/action |
| **Advertising** | Sell attention of users to advertisers |
| **Marketplace commission** | Take % of transactions between buyers/sellers |
| **Licensing** | Charge businesses to use your platform/API |
| **Data monetization** | Sell anonymized insights to third parties |
| **Physical goods** | Sell hardware or branded merchandise |
| **Partnerships / co-branding** | Revenue share with partner brands |

### Step 7: Recommend
Pick the best model(s) based on your analysis. Explain why it fits this segment, this product, and this goal.

## Common Question Examples
- "Describe different ways you would monetize a laundry business in Manhattan."
- "How would you monetize WhatsApp?"
- "How would you add a revenue stream to [free product]?"
- "Instagram is considering monetizing their Stories. How would you approach this?"

## Coaching Tips
- Segmentation is everything — the right model for power users may be wrong for casual users
- Don't just list models — connect each to a segment and explain *why* they'd pay
- Freemium works when the free tier creates a habit that converts; be explicit about the conversion mechanism
- Consider indirect monetization (data, partnerships) not just direct
