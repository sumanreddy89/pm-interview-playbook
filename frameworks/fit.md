# Fit / Recruiter Screen Framework

## What This Question Tests
Fit questions assess whether you're a compelling candidate for this specific role. The three core recruiter questions are: Tell Me About Yourself, Why do you want to work here?, and Why should we hire you?

---

## 1. Tell Me About Yourself

**What it is:** A 2–3 minute pitch connecting your background to the PM role.

**Structure:**
1. **Career theme** (1–2 sentences) — who you are, relevant to PM
2. **Supporting career highlights** — in chronological or reverse order; connect each to the role
3. **Why this is the logical next step** (1–2 sentences) — why you're uniquely qualified

**Do's:** Connect experience and accomplishments to the position. Be specific.
**Don'ts:** Don't recite a task list. Avoid clichés like "I am passionate about…" Don't be abstract.

---

## 2. Why Do You Want to Work Here?

**Structure:** "There are three reasons why I want to work for your company..."
- Reason 1: Specific product/technology that excites you (name something concrete)
- Reason 2: Relevant experience/skills you bring
- Reason 3: Mission/culture alignment (with a specific example)

**Tips:** Research the company deeply. Mention a specific product, team, or initiative by name. Show enthusiasm with a story, not platitudes.
**Don'ts:** Don't say "great culture" without specifics. Don't give generic answers.

---

## 3. Why Should We Hire You?

**Structure:** "There are three reasons I'm a great fit..."
1. Years of PM experience + specific success story
2. Technical/quantitative background relevant to this role
3. Personal passion for the product (show you're a user)

**Tips:** This is a sales pitch. Research the position requirements and target your examples to them. Support every claim with a real example.

---

## Evaluation Criteria
- Can you tell a compelling, coherent story?
- Do you understand the role and the company?
- Are you communicating genuine passion with specifics?
- Are you succinct (< 3 minutes for "Tell me about yourself")?

## Common Question Examples
- "Tell me about yourself."
- "Why do you want to work at [company]?"
- "Why should we hire you over other candidates?"
- "Walk me through your resume."
